Welcome to Radio Siamsa

Radio Siamsa is an online radio station from Dublin.
We broadcast only Irish tradional music, mainly Irish tunes, interspersed with Irish songs.

Our online radio station is funded entirely by the support of our listeners.
We don’t sell advertising, nor do we receive any government grants or other support. If you’d like to support our work, and ensure that we can continue to broadcast traditional Irish music to a worldwide audience, we’d be most grateful - just click here to make a donation.

Support The Station

Radio Siamsa is a volunteer run radio station and relies entirely on the support of its listeners for its operation.

We plays no ads, just music; you can listen to the best in traditional Irish music without interruption. We get no government or public body support funding – 100% of our funding comes from you, our listeners. Your support will pay for additional music CDs, server costs and bandwidth, ensuring the continued development of the station.

Could you help out with a donation? Every gift, large or small, makes a difference and helps keep the lights on and the server pumping out great tunes.
